a)

Given:
- P(B) = 0.01
- P(¬B) = 0.99
- P(Positive | B) = 0.95
- P(Positive | ¬B) = 1 - 0.90 = 0.10

P(Positive) = P(Positive | B) × P(B) + P(Positive | ¬B) × P(¬B)

P(Positive) = 0.95 × 0.01 + 0.10 × 0.99 = 0.0095 + 0.099 = 0.1085

P(B | Positive) = P(Positive | B) × P(B) / P(Positive) = (0.95 × 0.01) / 0.1085 = 0.0095 / 0.1085 = 0.0876

Answer: P(B | Positive) = 8.76%

P(B | Positive) = 0.50 = (0.95 × 0.01) / P(Positive)

0.50 = 0.0095 / (0.0095 + 0.99 - 0.99s) = 0.0095 / (0.9995 - 0.99s)

0.50 × (0.9995 - 0.99s) = 0.0095
